About me

I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Computing, Tokyo Institute of Technology (Tokyo Tech). My current research interests include but are not limited to, the FPGA-based embedded architecture for IoT applications. During my Ph.D. at Tokyo Tech, my research was in the general areas of data mining and knowledge discovery, with a particular focus on time-series analysis and sensor data processing for solving real-world problems involving low-resource and anomalous data.


For more information, please refer to my CV, and feel free to contact me.
